Bug Jazz

Bug Jazz is a collective 100% improvised recording/performing concept that eschews tonality to focus solely on vibes by blending aspects of elevator jazz and rock guitar music with ambient textures, dance rhythms, and emotional intuition. Bug jazz strives for an aesthetic that's 40% spiritual free jazz and 30% guitar solo played on a guitar shaped like a pizza. Ya dig???
