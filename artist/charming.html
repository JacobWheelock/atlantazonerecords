Charming

Charming is based in the countryside, where you can turn up your guitar and share your feelings with the world. Charming is for those who wish to see the beauty in the mundanity around themselves. The comfort and reliability of an ever-ticking clock. Take your anger, your sadness, your happiness, and hold it in your hands, Because it is yours to hold.

Tl;dr I downloaded a Big Muff plugin and bought a reverb pedal. Thank you for listening!
