Faced Out

Faced Out is an 8-piece emo/post rock band that formed in St. Louis, Missouri in 2019. While initially a 5-piece, Faced Out expanded and refined their sound for their sophomore album, adding a saxophone, violin, and trombone. The band draws on a wide variety of influences spanning from 2nd wave emo acts such as Mineral and I Hate Myself to modern post-punk such as Black Midi.
