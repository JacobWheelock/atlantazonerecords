Nimble

Nimble is a three piece emotional hardcore band from St. Louis. Consisting of Tim, Charlotte, and Alex this band channels the slosh of puddle splashes in wet cement, a drunk piss on the side of a brick building, and/or the unsteady heartbeat of an epileptic cat. Influenced by bands like Take it to Heart, Squint, and Have Heart, Nimble seeks to take the mid out of Midwest.
