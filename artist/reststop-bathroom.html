Reststop Bathroom

Songs about balconies. Songs about comparing sex to masturbation. What more could you want? Reststop Bathroom is a solo project by Faced Out’s Jasen. It is pretty much just run of the mill fifth wave post-/mu/ non-danceable bedroom pop infused lofi emo/skramz with Missouri characteristics. The type of stuff that will make you say “ew”.
