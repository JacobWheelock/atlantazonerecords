TunnelVision

Forged in a caldron of pure muthafuckin stank TunnelVision was born. TunnelVision is a four-piece Great Lakes Emo band originating from Cleveland, Ohio. TunnelVision formed in the Summer of 2021 but cemented their current line-up consisting of Ethan (vocals/guitar), Bri (vocals/guitar), Mitch (vocals/bass), and Tristan (drums) in Winter of 2023. TunnelVision's sound is drawn from various 4th & 5th Wave emo bands such as Glocca Morra, Title Fight, & Arms Length.
