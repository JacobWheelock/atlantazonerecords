Faced Out

Faced Out is an 8-piece emo/post rock band that formed in 2019 in St. Louis, Missouri. While initially a 5-piece the band expanded and refined its sound after its first album adding a saxophone, violin, and a trombone. The band draws on a wide variety of influences spanning from 2nd wave emo acts such as Mineral and I Hate Myself to modern post-punk such as Black Midi.
